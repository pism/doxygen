Detailed Description

Implements Dirichlet BC and the source term for a verification test from Tezaur et al, 2015 (), section 4.2.

Domain: [-50km, 50km] * [-1, 1] * [b, s].

Here b is the bed elevation, s is the surface elevation.

s = b + H0, H0 = 1000m.

Dirichlet BC are imposed at all the nodes along the lateral boundary.

Natural boundary conditions are used on the top boundary.

The basal boundary condition includes the sliding condition and a correction for the chosen manufactured solution.
