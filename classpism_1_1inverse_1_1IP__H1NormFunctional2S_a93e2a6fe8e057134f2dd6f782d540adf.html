void pism::inverse::IP_H1NormFunctional2S::gradientAt ( IceModelVec2S &  x,
IceModelVec2S &  gradient 
)
virtual

Computes the gradient of the functional at the vector x.

On an \(m\times n\) IceGrid, an IceModelVec \(x\) with \(d\) degrees of freedom will be \(d m n\)-dimensional with components \(x_i\). The gradient computed here is the vector of directional derivatives \(\nabla J\) of the functional \(J\) with respect to \(x\). Concretely, the \(i^{\rm th}\) component of \(\nabla J\) is

\[ \nabla J_i = \frac{\partial}{\partial x_i} J(x). \]

This vector is returned as gradient.
